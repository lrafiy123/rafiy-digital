The Porter Experience

Where Every Cut
Tells a Story

For over two decades, Porter has stood as one of New York City's most celebrated steakhouses — a place where the craft of butchery meets the art of hospitality.

We source exclusively USDA Prime beef from small family ranches across the American Midwest, dry-aged in our dedicated aging room for a minimum of 45 days. The result is a depth of flavor that no shortcut can replicate.

From the mahogany booths to the open-fire broiler visible from the dining room — every detail at Porter is intentional. This is not just dinner. This is New York.

— Chef James Callahan, Executive Chef

Your Event,

Porter's private dining rooms are among the most sought-after in Manhattan — perfect for corporate dinners, milestone celebrations, and everything in between.

01
The Vault Room
Seats up to 18 guests. Exposed brick, custom wine display, complete audio-visual system. Exclusive use of adjacent sommelier service.
02
The Callahan Room
Seats up to 32 guests. Named for our founding chef. Floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking 47th Street. Full buyout available.
03
Bespoke Menus
Work directly with Chef Callahan to create a custom tasting menu tailored to your guests and occasion. Dietary accommodations always honored.
