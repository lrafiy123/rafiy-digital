Who We Are

Built for
Results.
Not Excuses.

APEX was founded in 2017 by former D1 athletes and certified strength coaches who were frustrated with fitness studios that prioritized aesthetics over outcomes. They built something different.

Our 12,000 sq ft Tribeca facility features competition-grade equipment, dedicated Olympic lifting platforms, a full boxing ring, and a recovery zone with cold plunge and sauna. Every inch is built with one purpose: to make you better.

We don't believe in one-size-fits-all. Every member gets a baseline assessment, a personalized programming track, and quarterly goal reviews with a dedicated coach.
