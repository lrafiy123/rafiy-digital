About the Firm

A Law Firm Built on Principle

Gabay & Bowler was built on a bold premise: clients deserve sophisticated, experienced legal counsel — without the overhead, bureaucracy, and inflated billing of a major firm.

Founded in 2006 by Sari Gabay and Anne Marie Bowler — both Senior Litigators from Proskauer Rose LLP — the firm has spent nearly two decades proving that lean, focused, and brilliant beats big every time.

Identified by Women's Wear Daily as the go-to firm for retail clients, and described by The New York Times as "thriving and lean," the firm brings entrepreneurial energy and institutional pedigree to every engagement.

Clients work directly with founding partners — no associates buffering access, no surprise billing. Just experienced counsel on every call.

"There has to be another way to practice law and appreciate life — we found it."
Sari Gabay & Anne Marie Bowler, Co-Founders

Our Story

Why We Built Something Different

2006
Founded in NYC

After six years as litigators at Proskauer Rose, Sari and Anne Marie each stepped back — Sari to have her second child, Anne Marie to clerk for New York's Chief Judge. That distance gave them clarity: there had to be a better way.

They were most energized working directly with clients and solving problems creatively. So in 2006, they left — and built Gabay & Bowler from the ground up.

By 2011, The New York Times featured them as a thriving success story. Today they mentor other lawyers looking to make the same leap.
